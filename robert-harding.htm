Robert M. Harding

Email: rharding@ckpf.com

Robert ("Robb") Harding has more than thirty years experience representing and counseling individuals and companies in a broad range of matters relating to business, real estate, construction and commercial litigation.

Mr. Harding has been practicing law at Carr, Kennedy, Peterson and Frost since 1981, became a shareholder in the firm in 1986 and currently serves as the President of the firm. Mr. Harding has enjoyed the highest peer review rating, "AV Preeminent", from Martindale-Hubbell since 1996.
